Places Where these Fruits are famous

  • Apple: The United States (especially Washington state), China, and India are significant producers of apples.

  • Banana: Countries in Central and South America like Ecuador, Costa Rica, and Colombia are major banana producers. Additionally, India, the Philippines, and other tropical regions are known for their bananas.

  • Orange: Florida in the United States, Brazil, and Spain are well-known for their oranges. California is also a significant producer.

  • Strawberry: California in the United States is famous for its strawberries. Additionally, regions in Spain, Mexico, and the Netherlands are known for their strawberry production.

Production Percentage

  1. Apple:China leads global apple production, accounting for around 50% of the world's total. Other major producers include the United States (around 5-10%), India, and Poland.
  2. Banana: Approximately 50-60% of the world's bananas are produced in India, China, the Philippines, and Ecuador.
  3. Orange: Brazil is the largest producer of oranges, accounting for around 30% of global production. Other significant producers include the United States (mainly Florida), China, and Spain.
  4. Strawberry: The United States, particularly California, produces a significant portion of the world's strawberries, around 20-25%. Other major producers include Spain, Mexico, and Turkey.
